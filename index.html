About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Reda Harchid, a dedicated Front End Web Developer based in casablanca. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and have been passionate about web development since my early years.

With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I specialize in creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, beautiful, and intuitive designs. My goal is not just to write code, but to create digital experiences that leave a lasting impression.

Throughout my journey, I've had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects, ranging from personal websites to collaborative applications. My expertise extends to responsive design, cross-browser compatibility, and performance optimization.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me staying updated with the latest industry trends, experimenting with new technologies, and contributing to the web development community. I believe in continuous learning and strive to improve my skills with each project.
